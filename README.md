# Cadence

**AI-Powered Cold Email Outreach. Stop writing emails manually.**

![Cadence](frontend/assets/cadence-logo.svg)

## The Problem

Sales teams waste **4+ hours per day** writing cold emails:
- Manually personalizing each message (15-30 min per prospect)
- Copy-pasting templates (looks like spam)
- Sending one by one (slow)
- Forgetting follow-ups (lost deals)
- Missing replies (50% go unnoticed)

**Result**: Low reply rates. High time investment. Missed opportunities.

---

## The Solution

**Cadence** is an AI assistant that handles your entire cold email workflow:

1. **Writes personalized emails** — No templates, no placeholders. Real personalization.
2. **Sends from your Gmail** — Your domain, your reputation, your inbox.
3. **Automates follow-ups** — Day 3 and Day 7 automatically, if no reply.
4. **Detects replies** — Real-time notifications when prospects respond.
5. **Runs 24/7** — Works while you sleep.

**Result**: 10x faster. Better personalization. Zero missed replies.

## FAQ

**Q: Isn't this spam?**  
A: No. Emails are sent from your own Gmail account. We auto-detect bounces and remove invalid emails. Each message is genuinely personalized — no suspicious templates.

**Q: How do you access my Gmail without my password?**  
A: We use Google OAuth 2.0 (same as Google Drive). You authorize directly with Google. We get a token to send emails. Your password never passes through our servers.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. Data is encrypted at rest (Fernet encryption). Each user's data is completely isolated. Cross-tenant access is impossible by design.

**Q: How much does it cost?**  
A: We're launching on DemoDay. **First 50 teams get 3 months free.** After that: $99/month for unlimited campaigns.

**Q: Can I try it free?**  
A: Yes. Click "Try Free" below. No credit card required.

**Q: What if I don't like it?**  
A: Cancel anytime. Your data deletes immediately. Emails in your Gmail stay (they're yours).

**Q: Does this work with Outlook, Yahoo, custom domains?**  
A: Currently Gmail only. But if it's critical for you, let's talk.

**Q: How fast can I start?**  
A: 5 minutes total. Sign up → Connect Gmail → Upload CSV → Done.

**Q: Will my reply rate actually increase?**  
A: Yes. Because: (1) Better personalization = higher open rates, (2) Consistent follow-ups = more conversions, (3) You see every reply = no lost deals.

**Q: What if I send too many emails and get rate-limited?**  
A: Gmail has built-in rate limits (~500/day per account). We respect those limits and queue excess emails. Your account stays healthy.
